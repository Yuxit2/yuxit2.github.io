Urban Planning Research: Analysis, Equity, and Policy

1. Course Introduction

Course Overview

Urban planning decisions concerning transportation and land use play a central role in shaping accessibility and opportunities. This course provides a hands-on introduction to data-informed research in urban planning, focusing on transportation, land use, and equity.

Students will learn how to:

  • Frame planning research questions
  • Work with real-world datasets
  • Conduct spatial and non-spatial analysis
  • Translate findings into planning and policy recommendations
